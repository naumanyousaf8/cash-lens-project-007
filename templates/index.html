Our Story

Founded in 2024, CashLens.ai began with a simple vision: to create technology that serves everyone, especially those with visual impairments. Our team of AI specialists, accessibility experts, and developers came together to build a solution that makes currency identification simple and accessible.

Today, we're proud to offer the most accurate Pakistani currency recognition app available, helping thousands of users across the country gain financial independence.
